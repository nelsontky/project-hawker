🌟WALKING DOWN MEMORY LANE🌟

GOOD Morning Singapore. Reminders why we need to keep supporting the Hawkers. We the Last Gen unite.

When days were scorching hot the best reward to look forward to was a nice Ice Kachang Ball. I recall there was this Uncle that come around the estates to bring this delightful magic to the residents.

I'd be in my best behaviour on days I think he would come so that I can enjoy this awesome ice Kachang ball.

The Hawkers need our support. Today the best lives in our memories...
